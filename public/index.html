✦ Got questions?

Frequently asked

What hardware do I need? ▼
Just any device with a web browser. An old Android tablet for the waiter pad, a spare monitor or TV for the kitchen display, and a laptop for management. No special hardware required.
Is it hard to set up? ▼
Not at all. Create an account, add your menu items (takes about 10 minutes), and open the right URL on each device. Everything syncs automatically.
What happens if the internet goes down? ▼
The system is designed for local LAN operation, so it keeps working as long as devices are on the same network. For cloud deployments, we have offline fallback modes in development.
Can I migrate from my current POS? ▼
Yes — we offer free data migration from most major POS systems. Just export your menu and we'll import it for you.
Do you offer on-site setup? ▼
For Pro and Enterprise plans, we offer remote setup assistance. For Enterprise, we can do an on-site visit to get everything configured perfectly.
